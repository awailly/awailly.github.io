Aurélien Wailly

I am an engineer in Cloud computing security at Amazon, and previously Orange Labs. My primary research interest is the application of autonomic concepts to adapt security to cloud environments. I also study underlying virtualization security to evaluate the impact of security incidents, lower attack surface and provide remediation.
