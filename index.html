Aurélien Wailly

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in cloud computing security at Orange Labs and Telecom SudParis. My primary research interest is the application of autonomic concepts to adapt security to cloud environments. I also study underlying virtualization security to lower attack surface and provide remediation.
